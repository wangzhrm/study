Game Instructions

  • Clear Mode (Green border): Click to reveal a cell
  • Flag Mode (Red border): Click to place/remove a flag
  • Double-click on a revealed cell: If correct flags are placed around, reveal surrounding cells
  • Numbers indicate how many mines are in the 8 surrounding cells
  • Reveal all non-mine cells to win the game

Tip: Use "Clear" and "Flag" buttons to switch modes on tablets!
